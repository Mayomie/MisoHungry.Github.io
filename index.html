Deadline

Deadline is a co-op twin-stick shooter that revolves around making use of your environment to survive as long as possible as endless hordes of zombies attempt to kill you and any and all survivors of the infection. The viewpoint of the game is isometric, with the camera tracking to the direction the player aims. The game’s main focus is searching for resources to defend a single holding point while survivors escape from the floor you’re defending.

The point of the game isn’t to win, it’s not to lose. Players are deliberately thrown head first into a situation where they will be ultimately outnumbered by the growing force of infected, resources are few and far between resulting in a close situation where players are left to defend to the last man. The longer that the player can hold off the infected, the more survivors that can escape from the floor.

Unity 5 Engine, C#, AI, Lighting
